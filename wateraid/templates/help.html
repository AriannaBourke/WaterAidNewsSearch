How To Use The Search Portal


How to register and log in

Click 'Register' on the home page or 'Register Here' in drop-down on the top right of the page.

Fill out the short registration form and click the 'Register' button

You have now registered! The page will automatically redirect for you to log in to start using the search portal!

How to search for articles

Fill in the search bar with any word or term you like, for example 'Water, sanitation and hygiene'

The more specific the search terms entered the more targeted the results will be!

This will send a natural language query to Watson Discovery News and display the results on the search results page

Search results are displayed in descending order of relevance

Click the title of the article to see a preview

Click 'see more details' to view the top keywords, concepts, entities and categories. Also shown are the article sentiment and emotions.

In this page you can also click the article title to be taken to the full article page

How to perform an advanced search with filtering

Performing an advanced search is the best way to refine your results!

Click 'Advanced Search' on the right-hand side of the search results page

This presents option to filter by source, concepts, location etc.

Step 1: Enter any word or term you like, for example 'water shortage' into the search bar and click to add it to the filter panel

Step 2: If you wish to exclude WaterAid from the search, click the 'No WaterAid' button to add it to the filter panel. This will exclude all articles with WaterAid included in the title, keywords, concepts and entities

Step 3: Choose any combination of filters from the tabs

Click 'Submit' to perform the search

You can also use the map! This presents all the location entities which have been found in the search results

Click on one of the pins and choose filter by location to refine your results

How to save an article

Click 'see more details' on the article of your choice

Then click the green 'Save Article' button

To view all of your saved articles, click on the 'Account' drop-down at the top right-hand corner of the page

Click 'Saved Articles'

This will show a list of all of your previously saved atticles

You can search through the list using the search bar, where you can either type in the title of the article or keywords

If you wish to delete the article from your list, click 'Unsave Article'
